# Roman Society 1.1.326

Release date: 2026-06-16

A navigation and text-integrity audit, prompted by a misrouted Back button in the
intrigue menu.

## Navigation Fix

- Fixed the Back button in the intrigue/spy menu. "Build spy network" is reachable from
  two different parents: the crime menu ("Crimes, courts, and prison") and Roman power
  systems. The menu always returned to Roman power systems, so entering it from the crime
  menu and pressing Back jumped you to the wrong screen.
  - `openIntrigue` now remembers where it was opened from (`from: 'openCrimes'` vs the
    default Roman power) and routes Back, and all of its child actions
    (`buildSpyNetwork`, `startPoliticalConspiracy`, `useBlackmailHook`, blackmail
    results), back to the correct parent.
- Audited the rest of the politics, crime, religion, coalition, clientela, legion, and
  slave menus: every other sub-menu has a single parent and already returns to the right
  place. The intrigue menu was the only one with two parents and a hard-coded Back.
