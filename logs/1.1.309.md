# Roman Society 1.1.309

## 1.1.309 - House Survival And Member-List Coherence Pass

This release fixes a runaway house-extinction problem and makes the house
"Members" list match the house family tree.

### Fixed

- **Houses went extinct far too fast.** `ensureVisibleHouseMembers` simply skipped
  any house that had lost one gender (only men or only women left), so such
  households could never recover and slowly died out — exactly the "houses with no
  man and woman that dilute when the woman dies" behaviour. The growth pass now
  generates an adult of the missing gender to give the household a chance to
  continue, instead of skipping it.

- **House replenishment only ran on load, never during play.** The viability and
  growth pass (`ensureVisibleHouseMembers`) was only invoked from the startup
  `ensure()` path. During normal monthly play, houses only shrank (vanilla deaths)
  while `retireDeadHouses` ran every month, producing a steady stream of
  extinctions. A new bounded `sustainStrugglingHouses` pass now runs every month
  *before* retirement and restores a breeding pair to single-gender households
  (capped per tick), so houses can survive across turns.

- **A stack of extinction modals every tick.** `retireDeadHouses` pushed one modal
  per extinct house, so several houses dying in the same month produced several
  pop-ups. Extinctions in a tick are now consolidated into a single summary
  notification.

- **House "Members" did not show everyone in the house family tree.** The Members
  screen was built only from `house.memberIds`/`notableIds` (`visibleHousePeople`),
  which is narrower than the membership the family tree resolves. Relatives that
  appeared in the tree (for example the daughter of the player's uncle) could be
  missing from Members. The Members list now also seeds its candidates from the
  same `buildHouseTreeModel` the tree uses, so every non-guest tree member of a
  house appears in that house's Members. External "guest" spouses remain listed
  under their own real house, as before.

### Changed

- The runtime version is aligned to `1.1.309` (this also invalidates the
  per-version family-tree runtime cache).

### Notes / Not Confirmed

- `sustainStrugglingHouses` adds one adult of the missing gender; actual children
  still depend on the existing NPC romance/pregnancy simulation. This stops the
  forced dilution-to-extinction but does not guarantee growth on its own.
- Per-tick generation is bounded (sustain budget 4, plus the existing 260/420
  generated-character caps) to avoid Android stalls; on very large worlds recovery
  is gradual by design.
