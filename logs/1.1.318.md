# Roman Society 1.1.318

## 1.1.318 - Phase 1: real Roman place names in property/commerce notifications

This is the first, self-contained slice of the larger "more like Crusader Kings"
roadmap (see the design notes below). It adds real ancient-Roman place flavour to
property and commerce notifications, and lays the data foundation the later
land/feud systems will reuse.

### Added

- Place-name data in `config.js`: `romanRoads`, `romanDistricts`, `romanHills`,
  `romanLandmarks`, `romanRegions`, `romanEstateLocations` (real Roman roads,
  districts, hills, landmarks, regions/provinces, and estate descriptions).
- Helpers in `log_utils.js`: `romanPlace(kind)` and `romanEstateLocation()`.

### Changed

- Trade venture settlement and trade-deal notifications now mention a real Roman
  location (e.g. "A trade venture out of the Emporium docks pays your household ...",
  "Trade deal with House X over goods moving through the Forum Romanum ...").

### Notes

- Place lists are kept to Rome + Italy + long-held provinces so they stay broadly
  correct across the Republic / early Empire; precise per-year province gating is a
  later refinement (the year encoding in the base game is not yet confirmed).
- Runtime version aligned to `1.1.318`. No gameplay/stat changes; notification text
  only.

See `cor_society/DESIGN_CK_ROADMAP.md` for the full multi-phase plan (land feuds,
abduction-for-ransom, Senate -> Dictator -> Emperor progression, succession
contests, and war).
